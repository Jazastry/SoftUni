Integrity Constrsains
	- primary key constraint
		. the primary key can`t be null
		. it`s used to indentify rows in the table, have to be unique to 
		ensure that the rows contain different data and are not repeated
	- unique key constraint
		. the unique key have to be unique and not null
	- foreign key constraint
		. foreign key is unic for it`s own table and have to be valid existing
		value from related table, ensures that two tables are conected with existing
		valid rows
	- check constraint
		. are conditional expressions that ensure entered data coresponds ti it`s purpose
