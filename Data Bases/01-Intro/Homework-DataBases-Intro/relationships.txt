There are three types of relations in terms of relational database tables.
	- one to one
		one entity in the first table is connected with olny one entity from the other
		(like one country can have only one prezident for the current mandat) 

	- one to many
		one entiy in the first or second table can be connected to many entityes in the 
		other table
		(one year have many months but one month is part only of one year)

	- many to many
		many entityes fron one table can be in relations with many from other
		(one man can be fan of many football clubs, and one football club can have many fans)
